Play the Game

Who wants to be a Millionarie is a classic television triva game show, started in August 1999 and has continued to this day. This website is a non-profit fan recreation.


As a triva game Who wants to be a Millionarie is quite simple in concept. A question is displayed and four answers are displayed below. Think carefully about which answer is the corect one. Once you are stastified that you know the answer (or are willing to guess on it) click the corresponding answer. If you got it right you move on to the next question, if you got it wrong you lose. Answer 15 in a row and become a Millionarie (not really).
